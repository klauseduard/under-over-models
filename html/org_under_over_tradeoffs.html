Organizational Complexity: Underfitting vs. Overfitting Tradeoffs

Understanding the Visualization

This interactive tool demonstrates how organizational characteristics change as the structure moves from too simple (underfitting) to too complex (overfitting). The "balanced zone" represents an ideal middle ground where many aspects of organizational performance are optimized.

Move the slider to see how different organizational characteristics respond to changes in complexity. Notice how some characteristics improve with simplicity while others benefit from more structure, highlighting the inherent tradeoffs in organizational design.
